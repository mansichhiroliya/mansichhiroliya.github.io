Our Story

How we meet

Marilou was an Au Pair in New York for just one month when she decided to go out with some friends for the first time in New York City. That night, James was dragged out to a bar called “The 13th Step” by his friends. The two groups match on tinder social and they decided to meet up at this bar. The rest was history!

Our Story

The proposal

For Christmas James bought Marilou a private helicopter ride around NYC on January 5, 2018. After the flight around NYC we got off the helicopter and setup for what Marilou thought was a photo. James whispered into Marilou’s ear, got down on one knee, and he asked her to marry her. Marilou was incredibly surprised and said YES.
