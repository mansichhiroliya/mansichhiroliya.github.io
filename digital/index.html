GOOD eDIGITAL

As a full-service digital marketing agency, we understand the education industry better than most. We guarantee to provide our clients with a personalised and result-driven marketing campaign.

our differences

GOOD eDIGITAL is brought to you by Good Education Group, Australia’s largest education marketplace with over 20 years of experience targeting parents and students within Australia using traditional and digital marketing strategies.

Our online audience of over 4 million Australian parents and students will be accessible to our partners in order to run re marketing campaigns directly to an audience group who is actively comparing schools and courses on our websites.

  • 2 million views

  • social display google

Good Education Group (GEG) carries databases of all tertiary courses offered by Australian based education providers and maintains a database of all mainstream schools in Australia.
Publishing these databases across our platforms provides unique opportunities to collect web analytics against this data. Blending these databases against a range of other datasets such as Department of Education statistics provides further opportunity to generate rich insights and targeted digital marketing campaigns.
