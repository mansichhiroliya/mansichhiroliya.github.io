How does the “take fee from amount” work”?

If selected, your withdrawal amount will include the fee. For example, if the withdrawal amount is 2 an the fee is 0.001, your wallet will be debited 2, and you will receive 1.999 at your withdrawal address.

If unchecked, your wallet will be debited your withdrawal amount plus the fee (2.001) and you will receive the full 2 at the withdrawal address
